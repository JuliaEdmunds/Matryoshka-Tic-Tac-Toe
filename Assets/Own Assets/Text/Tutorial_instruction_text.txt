Drag and drop the highlighted piece on the highlighted cube to place it on the board. Once the piece is on board you cannot move it again.

The opponent and you will alternate placing your pieces on the board throughout the game.

Drag and drop the highlighted piece on the highlighted cube to crush your opponent's smaller piece (you can also crush your own pieces if you want to).

The player who succeeds in placing three of their pieces in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. Go ahead and place your piece on the board to win!
